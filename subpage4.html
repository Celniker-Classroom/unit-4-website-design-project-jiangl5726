Other instruments similar to guitar!

There are so many stringed instruments that are similar to guitars like the Lute, Mandolin, ukulele, banjo, and so much more!

Lute is different from guitars because it has a rounded back and shaped like a pear. The lute also usually have 15 strings and a different tuning system. Guitars usually has a flat back and fewer strings. One similarity though is the strings could be made out of nylon, and of course, they are both stringed instruments.
