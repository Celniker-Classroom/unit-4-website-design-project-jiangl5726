Classical Guitar!

classical guitar has a rich history in Spain and Latin American. It evolved from early stringed instruments like lute in the 15th and 16th centuries. A modern classical guitar usually have 6 nylon strings, wide neck, and hollow wooden body making a warm and mellow tone. This guitar is ideal for fingerstyle playing unlike steel string acoustic guitars. The nylon strings are gentler on fingers so its better for plucking techniques. Classical guitar is also usually used in classical music, flamenco, and Latin american folk music. Many musicians use this type of guitar in music producing and performing, some famous musicians include Willie Nelson, Leonard Cohen, Nick Drake, and more.


popular brands that sell classical guitars:

There are many brands that sell classical guitars, these include Alhambra, Cordoba, Kremona, and more. Alhambra is known for their rich, warm, balanced tone sound producing classical guitars. Their guitars have a smooth and resonant voice that suits classical styles beautifully. One of their famous classical guitar include their 1C model which is a good entry level classical guitar. Cordoba is one of the younger brands but quickly became one of the most popular classical guitar makers worldwide. Many of their models are inspired by historic Spanish designs, but still built with modern playability and consistency. One of their popular classical guitar is the Cordoba C5 which is a good starter to intermediate classical guitars having good traditional tone. Kremona is known for their handcrafted classical guitars by skilled builders using traditional technique who gives attention to every detail. One of their popular classical guitar is the Kremona Solea which is a highly regarded concert level classical guitar with a rich tone that deliver full, resonant sound good for classical performance. Their prices may range from $449 to $4,999.
