Electric Guitar! (My favorite type of guitar)

The electric guitar was developed in early 1930s. At that time many guitarists wanted to be heard over loud band esembles and large concert venues. Electric guitars defined many music genres that included early blues, rock and roll, and heavy metal. The first comercially successful electric guitar was called the Rickenbacker A-22 it was also nicknamed frying pan. The guitar was made in 1931 and it uses a magnetic pickup to convert string virbations to electric signals that are then amplifed. Many songwriters and bands across many genre of music perform with electric guitars as primary songwriting tool. some famous artists and bands include Prince, Kurt Cobain from Nirvana, Neil Young, Jimmy Page, The Strokes, and more!


many guitar brands and shops sell electric guitars like Fender, Gibson, Squire, Ibanes, and more. Fender has produced many famous guitars like the Telecaster, Stratocaster and more. Gibson is also famous for their electric guitars like the les paul standard which was known for its thick tone and looks. Squire is owned by fender and is reowned for making high value accessible versions of fender guitars. Their most famous series is the classic vibe series which has popular models like 50s/60s stratocaster and telecaster. Ibanez is also a famous brands known for selling electric guitars. their most famous is the RG series which is known as their flagship model for over 30 years. RG is famous for its versatile pickup configurations, double-locking termolo system, and looks. These are only the famous and well knwon bands that sell electrical gutiars. There are still many brands or shops out there that sell their own unique electric guitars.

to be able to play the electric guitar loud and with effects you will need a amp and effects. Amplifiers increase the guitar's signal strength. If you just play the electric guitar without it you'll notice the sound it produces is super quiet. Amplfiers usually have a preamp for tone shaping, a power amp to boost volume, and a speaker. You connect your electric guitar to an amplifer through a cable. Effects may also be used to change the sound the guitar produces. You can add it through physical pedals or digital modeling. There are many effects like distortion which boost signal and creat a gritty tone, modulation which make a swirling sound by changing pitch or phase, compresser which evens dynamics of playing making quiet notes loud and loud notes quieter, and more! To plug effects into your electric guitar you first connect guitar to pedal via cable then connect another cable from pedal to amp.
